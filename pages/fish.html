Aberrations

Aberrations are mutated variants of fish in DREDGE that are more difficult to find than their "normal" counterparts. They are described by the Fishmonger as 'corrupted', by the in-game text as 'grotesque' and is the first sign in the game that something is not right. The purple text box used in descriptions of Aberrated fish (same background color of inventory tiles for Aberrations, similar to Trophy fish) is described in the options as 'Important Color'.

Catching Strategies

Trying to catch Grotesque Fish from non-glowing, disturbed water can be irritating, due to the low chance of success and the requirement to perform the catching mini-game perfectly. However, if you leave the fishing minigame after make a mistake, the mistake is removed, allowing you another opportunity at catching an aberrated fish.

This is especially useful while trying to fill the encyclopedia, as certain fish spawn in few locations with only 1-2 fish per disturbed water patch (e.g. Devil Rays, Hammerhead Sharks, etc.).
